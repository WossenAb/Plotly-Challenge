What is the Belly Button Biodiversity Dataset?

In 2011 the Rob Dunn Laboatory launched an investigation foccused on studying the large varietiess of microbial life/bacteria that inhibit in the human navel. with in a few months of its launch, the research somehow garnered the attention of countless citizen scientists. Such a large public interest has contributed both to the popularity as well as the substantive scientific endavours of the study. For additional info on various aspects of the study, including the datasets used and the methodolgy, please visit the study sitehere.

For this dashboard, we used both Belly Button Data Sets 1 & 2. A jyupter notebook was used to convert and combine the CSV files collected for the resarch into a single sqlite dataset.

Viewing the dashboard locally requires a Flash application. A link to the various resources and orginal data used for the construction of this dashboard should be posted on github soon.Wondwossen Abatheneh (WossenAb)
